Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

What We Collect

BASELINE stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. Your scores, streaks, categories, and history never leave your phone.

Analytics

We use anonymous analytics (PostHog) to understand how features are used. This data cannot be tied to your identity. We track screen views, feature usage patterns, and crash reports. We never track the content of your categories or scores.

Subscriptions

Subscription purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status. RevenueCat receives an anonymous identifier — never your name, email, or personal data.

Push Notifications

All notifications are generated locally on your device. No push notification data is sent to or from any server.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time from Settings → Reset All Data. This permanently removes all local data. Since we don't store anything remotely, there is nothing else to delete.

Third-Party Services

• Apple App Store (subscription processing)
• RevenueCat (subscription management)
• PostHog (anonymous analytics)

Each service has its own privacy policy. None receive personally identifiable information from BASELINE.
